### What is HCI?

#### The Hallway of Interaction

> [!abstract] Leaving the Starting Room  
> Good job, you had the strength to get out and explore!
> 
> In this hallway, every door leads to a different part of the field. You might get lost but keep this map handy. Some doors lead to theory, where researchers study how humans think. Others lead to experiments, where real users are observed. Other doors lead to design, where interfaces are created, tested, and improved.
