> [!abstract] The Starting Point
> **Human-Computer Interaction** is the place where two worlds meet:  
> **Computer Science**, where systems are built, and **Cognitive Science**, where the human mind is studied.
>
> This entrance leads to five selected HCI areas based on the CS2023 view of HCI. Each room shows one important way researchers and designers study interaction between humans and computer systems.
>
